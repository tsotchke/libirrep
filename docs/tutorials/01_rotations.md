# Tutorial 01 — Rotations

Full content lands in M14. Topics covered:

- Constructing a rotation from axis-angle, Euler ZYZ, a quaternion, or a
  rotation matrix.
- Converting between every pair of representations.
- Composing rotations; inverse; geodesic distance; SLERP.
- Shoemake uniform random sampling on S³.
- Numerical gotchas: gimbal lock at β = 0/π; `rot_log` at angle = π.
